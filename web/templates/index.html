NCI Imaging Facilities Environment

The NCI Imaging Facilities Environment (NIFE) is an NCI-wide microscopy image management and analysis resource. From this website you can submit metadata for specimens to be imaged at one of the participating Intramural Imaging Facilities or access images on the NIFE image server.
